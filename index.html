Our Story

   Everything started by in 2007. We were eager to show San Antonio a side of the Mexican bakery and snacks culture so we decided to begin the adventure.
   With hope in our hearts, recipes and a mail truck that we adapted as a food truck, we started the journey. Since the beginning we realized that the people of this beautiful city not only loved Mexican cuisine but also the Mexican snacks. After a few months, our product demand was so overwhelming that we decided to invest in a bigger truck to be able to give a better service to our customers.
   And that is where "210 Ice Cream" was born, in the year 2008. The expectation started growing since the name reflected our committment of showing the whole city what our products were about.

   The next year our clientele kept growing and because we value our customers, we added a new 210 Ice Cream truck to the family.
   For a few years 2 trucks were enough, but we had to keep stepping up a few months after. By 2009, we had 6 trucks in service around San Antonio.
   Finally, 2010 we converted to our first 210 Ice Cream store! Downtown San Antonio was our witness in how 6502 S. Flores St. grew.
   In 2015 we had the fortune to opening our second store at 7126 Bandera Rd. to satisfy the customers in a new area.
   The road has not been easy, but 210 Ice Cream keep growing thanks to you! And we are incredibly grateful and committed to always offer our best service and products.
   Thank you, San Antonio. Forever!
